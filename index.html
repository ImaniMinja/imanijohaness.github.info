Welcome, I'm Imani Minja

A proficient, dedicated Data Analyst and Data Story teller

Ever since I was a kid, I was always fascinated by numbers. There was just something about the way they held stories, whispered truths and painted pictures of the world around me. As I grew, so did my passion for unravelling the tales hidden within data. It wasn't just about just numbers, it was about crunching numbers, it was about translating the heartbeat of information into meaningful narratives.

I developed the proficiency in the use of tools such as Python Programming for database querying, Microsoft Excel, SQL and Power Bi for analysis and pulling data from databases followed by communication of results. And through coursework, projects and self-driven learning, I have developed a solid foundation in data manipulation, statistical analysis and data visualization.

Other skills: • Graphics designing • Art

Beyond the graphs and charts lies a profound connection to the pulse of information and as a Data Analyst, I am on a life-long journey to give that heatbeat a voice.

Whether you want to discuss a project, exchange ideas or explore potential opportunities, feel free to reach out and let's see the endless possibilities of Data analysis.
